About

Vault is a website whose purpose is to bring more security to online meeting platforms. With the pandemic upon us, schools and organizations have scrambled to find an alternative to prevent learning loss, and to continue the same quality of education. However, there has been a persistent plague to the quality of education, Zoom Bombers. This is the common name that people refer to those who join a meeting that they are not invited to. With confusing settings and gimmicks to meetings, it has become easy for people to invade other meets. This is where Vault comes in. We provide a level of security that is easy to understand and follow. We use neural networks and facial recognition software to ensure that those here to learn and discuss are the same ones that are being admitted into the meeting. We help simplify technologies to those who are inexperienced, and make the dangerous place that is the internet, a safer place.
